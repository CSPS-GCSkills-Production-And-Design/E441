To: Director General
From: Director, IT Services
Subject: Reference

Given that Mr. Tremblay has worked for the Information Technology Services Directorate for nearly ten years now, and he has always demonstrated a keen interest in his work, I am of course satisfied with his performance. He has been able to stay abreast of all the latest technological advances. In addition to the vast knowledge that he has acquired, partly in this directorate, he has five years of relevant experience in the private sector.

Due to his engaging personality, Mr. Tremblay has always gotten on very well with our clients. In addition, Mr. Tremblay is a team player. His behaviour is beyond reproach, and his attitude toward his superiors and his work shows a maturity that has earned him the respect of everyone.

I am convinced that Mr. Tremblay will quickly earn the trust of his co-workers and supervisors, and that he will be an important asset to your team, given his immediate and unwavering commitment.
Answer choices:
Mr. Tremblay works in the private sector.
Mr. Tremblay’s duties consist of making technological advances.
Most of Mr. Tremblay’s knowledge has been acquired in the private sector.
Mr. Tremblay is highly regarded in his workplace.
