Objective

The power to relate causes and effects

The purpose of the module “The power to relate causes and effects” is to help you integrate linking words that will enrich the way you express yourself.  

The activities will enable you to practise the vocabulary and expressions for relating cause-and-effect statements and to improve your reading and listening comprehension and written expression.

It is suggested that you practise the cause-and-effect function in actual exchanges with your co-workers and friends.
