Activity 1.1

Read the text and indicate whether the statements that follow are true or false.

To: All program managers
From: Director General
Subject: Report

Hello,
                                                                                   
Given that I will be away from the office for three weeks, I think I should remind all program managers now that they need to submit a report on their activities at the end of each quarter.

For some of you, this date falls during your annual vacation. Since I cannot authorize you to postpone submitting this document to a later date, and because it is also out of the question to ask you to change your vacation dates, I am asking you to have one of your employees submit your report to your respective section heads. Seeing as some section heads will probably be on vacation themselves, you can, if necessary, submit your report to my replacement, Mr. Lachance.

Answer choices:
During the vacation period, all program managers must submit their reports to the Director General’s replacement.
Given that the Director General will be absent for three weeks, the program managers’ activity reports will have to be postponed to a later date.
In the Director General’s absence, the program managers must submit their reports to their section heads or to the Director General’s replacement if their section head is on vacation.
Because most of the section heads will be on vacation in the next three weeks, program managers must submit their reports to Mr. Lachance.
