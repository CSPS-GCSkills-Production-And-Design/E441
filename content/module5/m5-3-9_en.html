From: Frank Legend
Subject: Request for feedback

The following is an information kit for the presentations that will be given in the near future. The kit contains the schedule, list of objectives, important telephone numbers and a map of the building. As I mentioned on the telephone, I would like to have your feedback as soon as possible since I want to produce the final version in the next few days.

I know that you may not have enough time to review all the content of the kit as you are working on a project that must be completed by the end of the month. It’s just that I value your opinion. Consequently, it would be greatly appreciated if you could send me your comments on whichever parts you have time to review.

I apologize for rushing you with my request, but given that these kits are going to be widely distributed, I want someone reliable to review them. Thus, I am asking you.

If Frank does not have enough time to review all the content of the kits, he must concentrate on reviewing the opinion.
Josie wants to know if Frank thinks it is really necessary to include the schedule, objectives, telephone numbers and map of the building in the kit.
