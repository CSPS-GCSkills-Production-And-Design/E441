Example

Listen to the example and pay close attention to the expressions used for comparison.
Ali
So Charlene, how do you find your commute with the new bus route?
Charlene
It’s a bit longer, but much more convenient. I'm pretty happy. However, I’ll admit I had some concerns when the change was announced.
Ali
I think it’s much better. Now we pass by some shopping malls on the way home, and we can easily get off the bus to do our shopping. There is also a sports centre we can go to. Before the change, there wasn’t much to do on the route.
Charlene
Sure, but on the other hand, I liked that the old route went along the river. It was really beautiful and helped me to relax. In the summer, I could see the plants and trees along the water, and in the winter, the ice and snow on the trees. Now the drive is less interesting.
Ali
Yes, but even so, you can do a lot before getting home. Everything you need is on the way!
Charlene
Yeah, but still!
