Objective

The power to compare

“The power to compare” module is intended to help you better understand what a C-level comparison is so that you can make comparisons more easily.

This tool will allow you to use expressions of comparison and contrast, and to improve your listening and reading comprehension as well as your ability to express comparisons in writing.

However, it is suggested that you practise making comparisons in actual discussions with your colleagues. Feel free to do so by suggesting discussion topics that will allow you to compare items.
