I have attended a few conferences this month. Each event has been held in the new convention centre, which recently reopened after an extensive renovation. The first time I went there after the renovation, I was very excited to see the new layout. Of course, I was more excited to hear about the innovations that were taking place in my field.

Suggested answer:

He said that he had attended a few conferences that month. Each event had been held in the new convention centre, which had recently reopened after an extensive renovation. The first time he had been there after the renovation, he had been very excited to see the new layout. Of course, he had been more excited to hear about the innovations that had been taking place in his field.
