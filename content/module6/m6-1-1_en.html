Objective

The power to summarize

“The power to summarize” module is intended to help you better understand what a C-Level summary is so that you can summarize information more easily in a speech, conversation, meeting or document, or even summarize your own ideas.

This tool will help you incorporate phrases that are used in summaries as well as learn the rules of indirect speech because you are often required to report what other people have said when you summarize a situation. The activities will help improve your listening, reading and writing skills.

It is suggested that you practise summarizing during real-life conversations with your co-workers and friends.
