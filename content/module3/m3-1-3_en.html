Strategies

Some strategies to consider

  • Make sure you understand the topic before you start giving your opinion. If necessary, ask for clarification.
  • At the beginning, demonstrate to your listener that you understood the subject, take a clear position and elaborate.
  • Nuance your opinion using adjectives, adverbs and hypothesis to make your arguments more precise.
  • Consider the other point of view to confirm your arguments. Example: “Many people do not agree with what I have said. Rather, they . . . ”
  • Summarize your opinion with a clear signal that you are coming to the end using an appropriate expression to do so.
