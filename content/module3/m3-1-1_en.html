Objective

The power to give opinions

“The power to give opinions” module is intended to help you master the expressions used to give and qualify your opinion at the C level.

This tool will allow you to integrate expressions related to giving opinions through activities that focus on listening comprehension, reading comprehension and written expression.

It is suggested that you record yourself and listen to what you have said. It is also a good idea to practise giving your opinion with your colleagues to improve your fluency.
