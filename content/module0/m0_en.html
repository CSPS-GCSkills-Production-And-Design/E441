Power Builder

Power Builder is a C-level language maintenance tool. It contains six modules to help you master the linguistic elements that link ideas together to relate causes and effects, to compare, to ask for and give advice, to hypothesize, to give an opinion and to summarize.

This series offers theoretical and practical activities for listening comprehension, reading comprehension and written expression.
